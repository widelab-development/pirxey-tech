## How AI-Native is different from "we use AI for autocomplete"

Most agencies bolt AI onto an existing workflow — a Copilot subscription here, a ChatGPT prompt there. Speed doesn't change much because the bottleneck was never typing.

**AI-Native** redesigns the workflow around what AI is actually good at: exploring alternatives, scaffolding boilerplate, reviewing diffs, hunting bugs, writing tests, generating docs. Senior engineers focus on the parts AI still can't do well — judgment about trade-offs, system design, talking to humans, sniffing out incentive misalignments.

The result: 3–5× delivery velocity without quality regression. Often better quality, because seniors spend their day on the 20% of work that needs them, not the 80% of boilerplate.

## Engagement model

We don't sell hours. We sell milestones — clear deliverables with clear acceptance criteria. You know what you're getting and when. We know what we're being measured on.

For larger engagements: dedicated AI-Native squad (2–4 people), 8-week sprints, fixed scope per sprint, transparent burn-down. For prototypes: 4-week sprint, working software at the end, no excuses.
