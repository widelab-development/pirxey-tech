## When Rapid AI Prototyping is the right fit

- You have an idea but the technical risk is unclear and you need data fast
- You're tired of 6-month "discovery phases" that produce slides instead of working software
- You need to demo a working product to investors / pilots / internal stakeholders in a quarter, not a year
- You want to validate before committing to a 12-month roadmap

## What you get

A working prototype in 4–8 weeks. Real users can use it. Real data flows through it. It's not a clickable mock — it's deployable software.

If validation passes, the same team rolls into production hardening. No handoff overhead, no "phase 2 kickoff" theater. The team that built the prototype is the team that ships it.

## What we don't do

We don't sell hours. We don't write 80-page specs. We don't run a 6-week "discovery" before code touches a keyboard. We compress what other agencies stretch — because AI lets us, and because clients are tired of waiting.
