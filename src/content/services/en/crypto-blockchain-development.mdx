## Why crypto teams trust us

Most agencies treat smart contracts like web apps. They're not. A bug in a web app is a Sentry ticket. A bug in a contract is a $50M Twitter thread.

We treat security like air traffic control. Multiple eyes, formal review, test coverage that matters, and we never deploy a mainnet contract that hasn't passed an external audit when funds are at stake.

## What we won't do

We won't ship anonymous-team rugpulls. We won't help bypass regulations. We won't deploy on a chain we haven't shipped on before without saying so. We won't recommend tokenizing things that have no business being tokenized.

If your project has integrity, we'll match it. If not, we're not the right fit.
