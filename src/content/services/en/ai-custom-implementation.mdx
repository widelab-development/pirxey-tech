## Where we add the most value

AI agencies fall into two camps: prompt-engineering shops that promise the moon and deliver wrappers around GPT, and ML research consultancies that take 18 months to deploy a model. Pirxey sits in the middle — pragmatic, production-oriented, model-agnostic.

We pick the right tool: sometimes that's a fine-tuned LLM, sometimes a classical scikit-learn model, sometimes a rules engine that doesn't need AI at all. We're judged on whether your workflow gets better, not whether the solution is "AI-powered" enough to justify a press release.

## Engagement model

Discovery sprint (2 weeks) to map the data, define success metrics, and de-risk. Then a focused build sprint (4–8 weeks) to deliver a working pipeline. Then ongoing optimization, retraining, and monitoring.
